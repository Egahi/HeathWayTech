Bricks are used to 
1. build the walls of buildings (e.g houses, industrial buildings etc)
2. build the walls of drainage systems
3. build the borders of foundations for buildings
4. create elevated platforms for placing containers
5. create asthetically pleasing finishes for walls which would not be painted


A blanket is used to
1. Keep the body warm in cold weather
2. put out fire by cutting the oxygen flow to the flames
3. lay beds in the absence of a proper bed cover
4. provide a manageable platform to lie on in the absence of a bed
